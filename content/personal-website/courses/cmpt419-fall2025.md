# CMPT 419: Collective Action and Public Interest AI

This graduate-level special topics course examines the relationship between AI systems and collective action, with a focus on how communities can shape AI development and deployment.

## Course Objectives

- Understand the theoretical foundations of data leverage and algorithmic collective action
- Analyze case studies of successful and unsuccessful attempts to influence AI systems
- Design interventions that align AI systems with public interests
- Critically evaluate proposals for AI governance and regulation

## Format

The course combines lectures, paper discussions, and hands-on projects where students will develop tools or conduct research related to public interest AI.
