# CMPT 419: Human- and Data-centric AI (Spring 2024)

First offering of the human- and data-centric AI course.

## Course Structure

Four thematic modules covering:
1. Administration and foundations
2. Data valuation
3. Online platforms and UGC ecosystems
4. Data governance and markets

## Grading

- 10% Reading responses
- 20% Coding assignments (Python, JavaScript)
- 30% Tests (quizzes + final exam)
- 40% Final project

## Policies

- Generative AI tools permitted with proper attribution
- One free late submission per assignment type
