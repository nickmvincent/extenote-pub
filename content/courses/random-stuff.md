# Random Useful Stuff for Research Students

An informal collection of practical skills, tools, and wisdom for computing science research students.

## Why This Exists

Graduate school involves learning many skills that aren't taught in formal courses. This is a living document of useful things I've picked up or wish I'd known earlier.

- Task management systems
- Note-taking for research
- Reference management
- Time blocking and deep work

### Technical Tools

- Docker basics for reproducibility

### Research Skills

- Experiment tracking
- Data management
- Reproducibility practices

### Academic Life
- Conference attendance tips
- Networking strategies
- Giving good talks
- Handling rejection

### Career
- Building a research profile
- Industry vs. academia decisions
- Interview preparation
- Negotiation basics

### Wellbeing
- Managing imposter syndrome
- Work-life balance in grad school
- Building a support network
- Knowing when to ask for help
