# CMPT 419 - Human- and Data-centric AI (Spring 2025)

This course explores human-centered and data-centered approaches to AI.

## Course Modules

1. **Module 1 (Weeks 1-4):** Introduction to Different Frameworks for Human-Centered and Data-Centered Work
2. **Module 2 (Weeks 5-7):** Technical Work in Data Valuation and Scaling
3. **Module 3 (Weeks 8-10):** Online Platforms and Content Ecosystems
4. **Module 4 (Weeks 11-13):** Frontiers in Data Governance: Voting, Markets, and More

## Course Format

- Heavy reading and discussion component
- Weekly reading responses
- 4 coding/data analysis assignments (one per module)
- In-class quizzes (one per module)
- Final project (40% of grade)
